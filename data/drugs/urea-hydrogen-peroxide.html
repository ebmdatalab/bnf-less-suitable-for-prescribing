Indications and dose

Softening and removal of earwax

To the ear

  • For Adult

    (consult product literature).

Patient and carer advice

The patient should lie with the affected ear uppermost for 5 to 10 minutes after a generous amount of the softening remedy has been introduced into the ear.

Less suitable for prescribing

Urea-hydrogen peroxide ear drops are less suitable for prescribing.

Medicinal forms

There can be variation in the licensing of different medicines containing the same drug.
