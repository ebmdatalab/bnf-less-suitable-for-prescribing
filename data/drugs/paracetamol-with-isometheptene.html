Indications and dose

Treatment of acute attacks of migraine

By mouth

  • For Adult

    2 capsules, dose to be taken at onset of attack, followed by 1 capsule every 1 hour if required, maximum of 5 capsules in 12 hours.

Contra-indications

Acute porphyrias ; glaucoma; severe cardiovascular disease; severe hypertension

Interactions

Individual interactants:

  • Paracetamol
  • Isometheptene

Patient and carer advice

Patient counselling is advised (dosage).

Less suitable for prescribing

Isometheptene with paracetamol is less suitable for prescribing (more effective treatments available).
