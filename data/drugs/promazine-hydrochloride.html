Indications and dose

Short-term adjunctive management of psychomotor agitation

By mouth

  • For Adult

    100–200 mg 4 times a day.

Agitation and restlessness in elderly

By mouth

  • For Elderly

    25–50 mg 4 times a day.

Contra-indications

CNS depression; comatose states; phaeochromocytoma
